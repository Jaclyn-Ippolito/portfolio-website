About

My Story

I’m a UI/UX Designer with a background in visual art. I’ve always had a passion (bordering on obsession) for visual balance and order. I’m also a strong believer in combining art and science to better society. Together, these qualities form the cornerstone of my path to UI/UX Design.

When I’m not working on designs I enjoy all things art, checking out new places, antiquing, stand-up comedy, and being outside as much as possible.
